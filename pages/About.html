About Page

about Columbina

Columbina is known for her wit, productiveness, and association with the Fire Lady. She is a master of disguise and a skilled manipulator.

about Durin

Durin is a dragon from Simulanka, awakened by song. He is tied to the Fire Lady and the Simularkan legacy.

about Nicole

Nicole Reeyn, also known by her codename "N," is a member of the Hexenzirkel. She is said to offer help to those in need of guidance, which led her to be known by other members of the Hexenzirkel as the "guide who will never get lost." She has a habit of speaking directly into people's minds without warning.

about Alice

Alice, also known by her codename “A,” is the mother of Klee, a famous adventurer who authored the Teyvat Travel Guide, and the founder of the Hexenzirkel, as well as its Elder. She is a polymath whose skills include alchemy, astrology, engineering, magic, and medicine.

about Sandrone

Sandrone, also known by her codename "Marionette," is the Seventh of the Eleven Fatui Harbingers and a member of the Adventurers' Guild. She is currently stationed in Nod-Krai to oversee the Kuuvahki Experimental Design Bureau. She is almost always accompanied by Pulonia, a modified Ruin Machine.

about Aino

Aino is a playable Hydro character in Genshin Impact. She is a child prodigy and the founder of the Clink-Clank Krumkake Craftshop who created Ineffa and many other intricate machinery. She is an orphan and sees her creations and screws as her "family."

about Flins

Kyryll Chudomirovich Flins is a playable Electro character in Genshin Impact. A Lightkeeper of Nod-Krai, lone guardian of the lighthouse and graveyard on the Final Night Cemetery. Though appearing enigmatic and cold, he is a cultured and courtly gentleman, and one of the last of the Snowland Fae still in Nod-Krai.

about Lauma

Lauma is a playable Dendro character in Genshin Impact. As the Moonchanter of the Frostmoon Scions, Lauma is a well-respected and wise figure within the group, and trusted by all manners of wildlife. She is immeasurably kind and seeks to find a calm resolution to any conflict; though her steel will reveal itself should people mistake her benevolence for acquiescence.

about Varka

Varka is the current Knight of Boreas and Grand Master of the Knights of Favonius.Half a year before the start of the Traveler's journey,he departed on an expedition with eighty percent of the Knights of Favonius' manpower, leaving Jean as the Acting Grand Master in his absence.

about Nefer

Nefer is a playable Dendro character in Genshin Impact. Hailing from Sumeru, she is the head of the Curatorium of Secrets and a member of the Voynich Guild. She is incredibly calculating and knowledgeable to the point people speculate she can predict the future.
